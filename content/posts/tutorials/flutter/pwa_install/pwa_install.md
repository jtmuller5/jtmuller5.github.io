# PWA is the Way
Over the past month its become clear that Apple thinks it owns all apps on the App Store:
 - [Apple cracks down on NFT functionality, social post boosts with App Store rules](https://techcrunch.com/2022/10/25/apple-cracks-down-on-nft-functionality-social-post-boosts-with-app-store-rules/)
 - [Why Spotify is Really Mad at Apple Over 'Unfair' Fees relate to Big New Feature](https://loudwire.com/spotify-apple-app-store-audiobook-fees-unfair/)
 - [Apple flexes its control over the App Store](https://www.theverge.com/2022/10/27/23426993/apple-app-store-rules-guidelines-ads-changes-flexes-control)

Whether your app sells audio books, social post boosts, or basically any other in-app commodity, Apple believes you owe it 30% for the privilege of using its platform.

This is obviously unfair. First, they prohibit side-loading so developers can only deliver iOS apps to Apple users through the App Store. Then, they proceed to charge a 30% fee to do so.
