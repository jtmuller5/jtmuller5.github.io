What is the app-layer I care about? Creating software is fun, the feeling of creation is rewarding, but somehow it feels less than important in the grand scope of things. Its more so the case when the software is for software developers. We create minds to replace our minds and squeeze the joy of creation into dollops of loops and if statements.

What is the app-layer for idea storming or riffing or creation? What can I log into each morning to plan my day with uber effectiveness and mod to feel better or think faster or think differently?

A headquarters.

Is it VS Code still? 

Copilot and most AI tools augment users, accelerating the tasks the user asks for better than they perform the tasks the user asks about. They accelerate the easy parts if "accelerate" meant to make the entire window of execution one consistently fast speed. The agents are built to assist those with agency. You need to know what you want to build. And if you don't, the tools are a loaded handgun radiating an aura of potential that drives one mad.

We need an app-layer for ideation that aids the user in steering their thoughts towards the star of their choosing or a star of a certain sparkle. Wake up, plan for success, plan for creation, think differently, eradicate barriers and brick walls in the ruble of your mind and cast the streets in gold. We need a platform where anyone can share their hunches and lines of thought. The platform would encourage interaction more than X, Bluesky, Threads, Instagram, or YouTube. The fuel of the feed would be half algorithmic and half realtime biological.
