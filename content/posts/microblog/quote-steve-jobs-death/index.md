I watched the [Steve Jobs biographical drama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jobs_(film)) this weekend and was surprised for the fifth time that Jobs was not really a technical person. Compared to the layman, sure, but his entire career and the rise of Apple was due mostly to his obsession with craftsmanship.

> Remembering that you are going to die is the best way I know to avoid the trap of thinking you have something to lose. You are already naked. There is no reason not to follow your heart

Steve Jobs
